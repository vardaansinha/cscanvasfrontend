iFrame Science

At HawkHub, we utilize the power of iFrames to deliver seamless and interactive content directly to your browser. This allows for a dynamic learning experience, where content can be tailored and adjusted in real-time based on user interactions.

Interact with Teacher's Assignments

Students can directly interact with assignments provided by their teachers. This two-way interaction ensures that students get immediate feedback on their work, and teachers can gauge the understanding and performance of their students in real-time.

Machine Learning Predictions

With our advanced machine learning models, teachers can get insights into the potential future performance of their students. Using linear regression models, we predict the trajectory of a student's grades, allowing for timely interventions and support.
